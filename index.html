Our Story

Skinship Beauty is your go-to aesthetics clinic in Cainta, Rizal, offering relaxing and professional care. Since opening in 2022, we’ve grown from body contouring to a full range of services—facials, HydraFacial, Black Doll treatments, nail care, massages, and hair removal—helping you look and feel your best.

Skinship Beauty is an aesthetics clinic that offers non-invasive procedures, skin care services, and spa and massage treatments. Inspired by passion and a desire to help others improve their self-esteem, Kim Tanhueco opened Skinship Beauty. It first opened on February 26, 2022, in Primark Town Center Mall, Cainta, Rizal, as a start-up skin care clinic. Starting with body contouring using Exilis machines, Skinship Beauty quickly expanded to include nail care, massages, and advanced facial treatments. Currently, they also offer HydraFacial and Black Doll treatments as well as hair removal services.
